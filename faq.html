Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a snowflake?

Simply type a name or short phrase on the main page, adjust the arms, font, and color, and your snowflake appears instantly. You can then download it as a PNG or SVG.

Can I use my snowflake commercially?

Personal and classroom use is free. For selling printed items, please contact dna.art89@gmail.com for licensing info.

Why are my downloads large?

PNG and SVG files are exported around 350 DPI so they print clearly on shirts, cards, and other projects.

Can I request new fonts?

Yes! We love adding new options. Email us with your favorite font suggestions at dna.art89@gmail.com.

Why can’t I see the snowflake right away?

Occasionally fonts take a moment to load. Try refreshing once or changing to another font to re-render.

Where can I share my designs?

Tag us on Facebook or use #MyNameSnowflake — we love seeing your creations!
