Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a crisp, printable image?

Use Download PNG for a ~350-dpi export (about 2800×2800). Use Download SVG if you want a vector file for Cricut/Illustrator/Canva.

My SVG looks tiny in my editor—what do I do?

It’s sized with pixel dimensions; just scale it up in your editor and it will stay perfectly sharp.

Why does Print show only the snowflake?

We hide the rest of the page when printing so you get exactly the snowflake centered on one sheet.
