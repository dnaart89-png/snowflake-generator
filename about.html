About Nameflake Studio

We turn words into art — elegant, printable snowflakes you can use on ornaments, cards, shirts, coloring pages, weddings, and more. Each snowflake is generated from your text, then mirrored around the circle to create a design that’s uniquely yours.

Create Your Snowflake

Our Story

Family-built, memory-focused

Nameflake Studio began as a simple idea: capture a name or a phrase that matters and shape it into something you can hold, color, print, or gift. We wanted a tool that’s beautiful, fast, and easy for families, teachers, and makers.

“Each snowflake is uniquely yours.”

How it works

From text → snowflake

  1. Type a word or short phrase (e.g., David Loves Angie).
  2. Choose your font, color, and arm count.
  3. Adjust size and spacing for the style you like.
  4. Download as high-res PNG (≈350 DPI) or SVG for vector editing.
  5. Print, color, craft, or share!
